The Hilliard Standard

Uncompromising in Every Detail

Heritage

Rooted in centuries of horological tradition, every Hilliard timepiece honours the masters who came before while forging its own legacy.

Precision

Every movement is assembled by hand, individually tested to COSC chronometer standards, and certified to perform within ±2 seconds per day.

Materials

We source only the finest — conflict-free diamonds, 18k gold, double-curved sapphire crystal, and hand-stitched leather from the world's premier tanneries.

Our Story

Born from a Passion
for Perfection

Hilliard Time was founded on a singular conviction: that a watch should be more than a timekeeping instrument. It should be an heirloom, a companion, and a statement of who you are.

Every decision — from the selection of raw materials to the final regulation of the movement — is guided by an unrelenting pursuit of excellence. We make fewer watches by design, because true luxury has never been about volume.

"A Hilliard watch is not bought. It is chosen."
